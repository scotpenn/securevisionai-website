About Us

Our Team

Our Management Team is a well-balanced collection of experienced Executive, R&D, Sales & Marketing, Logistics, and Customer Service professionals committed to delivering the best possible products and support to our Distributors, Retailers, and End-Users.

SecureVision AI supplies a wide range of state-of-the-art Cameras, Monitoring, and Backup Power Products. We sell exclusively to Distributors and Retailers to offer to end-users.

Camera and Monitoring Manufacturing

SecureVision AI products are manufactured and certified exclusively for the Canadian market by Shenzhen interthings Technology Co. Ltd. in its factory located in Dongguan, China. Interthings specializes in Smart-Home security camera items such as indoor & outdoor IP cameras, and smart doorbells, and supplies these products to the USA, Europe, Asia, and Worldwide.

Our exclusive relationship with Interthings provides SecureVision AI the ability to offer competitive pricing, and to ship to our Distributors/Retailers within 60 days of order date.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI is revolutionizing camera technology by improving image quality, enhancing user experience, and expanding the creative possibilities for home security products. SecureVision AI's current products use AI to enhance facial recognition, motion detection, user interface, and object tracking.

AI helps to identify unusual activities or behaviors in surveillance footage, alerting users to potential security threats. Our future products, currently under development, include further AI technology features to further improve security in homes, businesses, and vehicles.
