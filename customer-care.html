Warranty Policy

1 Year Warranty – End-Users

SecureVision AI will replace any defective product that is returned to the SecureVision AI Warehouse within 1 Year of the date of Receipt by the End-User.

Warranty Return Process – End-Users

All of SecureVision AI's Products will be covered by our 1 Year Warranty Policy:

  • Replacement if returned within 1 Year of the end-user original receipt of product date.
  • Receipt of sale and proof of delivery date must be included with the defective product.
  • Defective items must be shipped to SecureVision AI's Vancouver warehouse (#235 7080 River Road, Richmond, BC, V7E 5L4), at end-user expense, post-dated within 1 Year of receipt of the product by the end-user.
  • Defective items must be returned in the original packaging, including all manuals, instructions, and accessories.
  • Defective items will be inspected for defects and if the product is in fact defective, and not damaged by the customer, a replacement product will be sent to the end-user within 30 business days by SecureVision AI.

Return Policy – Partner Distributor/Dealer/Supplier

Within 30 days of receipt of delivery of a shipment, the SecureVision AI Partner can return any damaged products to SecureVision AI for a full Replacement or a full Credit towards the next order, whichever suits the Partner best.
