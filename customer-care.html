Warranty Policy

1 Year Warranty – End-Users

SecureVision AI will replace any defective product that is returned to the SecureVision AI Warehouse within 1 Year of the date of Receipt by the End-User.

Warranty Return Process – End-Users

All of SecureVision AI's Products will be covered by our 1 Year Warranty Policy:

  • Replacement if returned within 1 Year of the end-user original receipt of product date.
  • Receipt of sale and proof of delivery date must be included with the defective product.
  • Defective items must be shipped to SecureVision AI's Vancouver warehouse (#235 7080 River Road, Richmond, BC, V7E 5L4), at end-user expense, post-dated within 1 Year of receipt of the product by the end-user.
  • Defective items must be returned in the original packaging, including all manuals, instructions, and accessories.
  • Defective items will be inspected for defects and if the product is in fact defective, and not damaged by the customer, a replacement product will be sent to the end-user within 30 business days by SecureVision AI.

Return Policy – Partner Distributor/Dealer/Supplier

Within 30 days of receipt of delivery of a shipment, the SecureVision AI Partner can return any damaged products to SecureVision AI for a full Replacement or a full Credit towards the next order, whichever suits the Partner best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our 1-year warranty covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship. This includes camera hardware, power systems, and mounting accessories. The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, weather exposure beyond product specifications, or normal wear and tear.

We provide detailed installation guides and video tutorials for all our products. For complex installations, we recommend consulting with a local security installer. Our customer support team is available to assist with installation questions via phone or email.

Most of our cameras support standard protocols and can integrate with existing security systems. Our products work with popular NVR systems and support ONVIF standards. Contact our technical team for specific compatibility questions regarding your current setup.

Our outdoor cameras support multiple power options including PoE (Power over Ethernet), DC power adapters, and our SecurePower battery systems. The SecurePower line provides reliable backup power and is ideal for locations without nearby electrical outlets.

Our cameras support both local storage (SD card/NVR) and cloud storage options. Cloud storage plans include 7-day, 30-day, and unlimited retention options with end-to-end encryption. You can access recordings through our mobile app or web portal from anywhere.

We offer comprehensive support including phone support (604) 340-1559, email support, live chat, and an extensive knowledge base. Our technical support team is available Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM PST. Premium support plans include priority response and remote configuration assistance.
